## RTP Account Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. Separate Kmedtest and KJM from RTP — any active non-RTP campaigns in the same Meta Business account must be paused before RTP restructuring
2. Sales objective only — never Awareness objective for product campaigns
3. Minimum $35 CAD/day per campaign — below this, learning phase never exits
4. Maximum 3 simultaneous campaigns at $100-120/day budget level
5. TOFU creative must be Educational or Authentic — not Commercial
6. Retargeting must exclude purchasers — always
