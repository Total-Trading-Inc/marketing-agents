## KMEDTEST — Photography Direction

### Core principles
- People first — calm, focused energy. Not performed happiness. Not clinical.
- Korea as feature — Seoul architecture, morning light, modern hospital environment as background detail
- Color temperature: 4500–5500K (warmer than clinical, cooler than travel/lifestyle)
- Avoid: doctor in white coat, abstract medical graphics, equipment without people, stock photo energy

## RTP TENNIS — Photography Direction

### Core principles
- Dynamic sports photography — real court environments, not studio setups
- Players in motion or post-point (authentic, not posed)
- Product integration: racquet in hand, sock visible in stride, heel impact during movement
- Color temperature: 5500–6500K for outdoor clay courts (natural daylight); 4500–5000K for indoor/evening
- Avoid: obvious stock photography energy, too-perfect poses, white studio backgrounds
