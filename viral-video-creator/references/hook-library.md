# Hook Library — 40+ Templates by Type and Funnel Stage

## TOFU Hooks (Awareness — no brand reveal, broad appeal)

### Curiosity Gap (TOFU)
1. "Most [audience] don't know that [surprising fact]..."
2. "The reason [common painful experience] keeps happening — and it's not what you think."
3. "Nobody talks about [mechanism that causes the problem]. Here's why they should."
4. "There's a [product category] designed for [audience] that most people have never heard of."
5. "What [expert/professional] knows about [pain point] that recreational players don't."

### Pain Mirror (TOFU)
6. "That [specific sensation] after [activity]? That's not [what they think it is]."
7. "[X] years of [pain/problem]. Until I understood what was actually causing it."
8. "If you play [sport] 3+ times a week, this is happening to your [body part]."
9. "[Pain/problem] that won't go away — even after rest, physio, and braces."
10. "You don't realize how much [pain/limitation] is affecting your [performance/life] until it's gone."

### Contrarian (TOFU)
11. "[Common piece of advice] won't fix [problem]. Here's what actually will."
12. "Stop [commonly recommended thing]. It's making [problem] worse."
13. "The [product type] everyone uses does almost nothing for [the actual problem]."
14. "[Pain condition] isn't a [what people think it is] problem. It's a [actual mechanism] problem."
15. "Your [expensive solution] isn't working because it's targeting the wrong thing."

## MOFU Hooks (Consideration — mechanism, proof, expert credibility)

### Bold Claim (MOFU)
16. "This [material/technology] absorbs [specific %] of [impact type]. Here's what that means."
17. "[Specific number]+ athletes use this. Here's exactly why."
18. "The same material used in [prestigious application] — on your [product]."
19. "[Expert credential] built this because [specific insight from expertise]."
20. "[Proof point with specifics]: [number], [result], [timeframe]."

### Transformation (MOFU)
21. "I played through [pain] for [duration]. Then I understood the mechanism."
22. "From [bad state] to [good state] — here's the specific thing that changed."
23. "[Pain condition] → no pain in [timeframe]. The one change: [mechanism]."
24. "Before: [problem described specifically]. After: [resolution described specifically]."
25. "What happened when I stopped [wrong solution] and started [correct solution]."

### Objection Kill (MOFU)
26. "No, it's not [common objection/misunderstanding]. It's actually [correct mechanism]."
27. "[Thing people try that doesn't work] treats the symptom. [Solution] treats the source."
28. "You've probably tried [common thing]. Here's why it only partially works."
29. "The difference between [product category] and what most people use: [specific mechanism]."
30. "If [common solution] worked, [problem] wouldn't still be a [scale of problem]."

## BOFU Hooks (Conversion — risk reversal, social proof, hard CTA)

### Social Proof (BOFU)
31. "[Large number]+ [audience type] use this. Here's what they're saying."
32. "[Specific testimonial outcome]: '[quote from customer]'"
33. "Official [partner/sponsor of prestigious thing] — here's what that means for you."
34. "[Expert] trusted this for [their athletes/clients]. Now available to everyone."
35. "[Number] reviews. [Rating]. One common theme: [outcome]."

### Objection Kill + Risk Reversal (BOFU)
36. "Still on the fence? [Answer the exact hesitation]. + [Risk reversal]."
37. "If it doesn't [specific outcome], return it. [Terms of guarantee]."
38. "The risk isn't trying it. The risk is another [timeframe] of [problem]."
39. "[Price] to [specific outcome]. Or [cost of the problem continuing]."
40. "30-day return. No questions. Because we're certain [specific claim]."

### Urgency (BOFU — only use if urgency is real)
41. "[Specific real deadline or scarcity]: [what happens after]"
42. "Limited stock of [product] — [specific number] left."
43. "[Seasonal context]: this is the best time to [start/fix/protect] before [season event]."

## Kmedtest — Recommended Hooks by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Approach | Example |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | Lead with North American problem (NOT Korea or brand) | "Getting a comprehensive health checkup in Canada takes 18 months on average." |
| TOFU | Cost contrast without naming service | "A single MRI scan in the US costs $2,499. Most people don't know there's an alternative." |
| MOFU | Mechanism + proof after establishing the problem | "103 tests. One morning. Hanshin Medipia — JCI accredited, 100,000+ annual checkups." |
| BOFU | Objection kill + specific process detail | "Flying to Korea for a health checkup sounds complicated. Here's exactly what happens." |

### Kmedtest anxiety-acknowledgment pattern (REQUIRED for all content)
Before resolving the problem, always acknowledge the anxiety:
- "Flying abroad for healthcare sounds like a big leap."
- "You might be wondering if this is really as straightforward as it sounds."
- "The idea of navigating a foreign hospital is a real concern."
THEN resolve it with specifics, not reassurance.
